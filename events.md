## EDITS Webinar #11 – Integrating Demand-side Solutions for Accelerated Decarbonization  
I was invited as a **speaker** at the  
**[EDITS Webinar #11](https://www.eiee.org/event/edits-webinar-7/)** on  
*“Integrating Demand-side Solutions for Accelerated Decarbonization of the Power Supply System:  
Insights from Bangladesh and Thailand”*, held on **November 5, 2025**.

The session was moderated by **Jubair Sieed** (RITE, Japan) and organized by the  
**Energy Demand changes Induced by Technological and Social innovations (EDITS)** project.
