<div class="event-section">
  <h2>ADB Youth Capacity Workshop – Bangkok, February 2025</h2>

  <p>
    Led the <strong>winner team</strong> at the 
    <em>ADB Youth Capacity Workshop on Developing Youth Solutions for Urban Climate Resilience</em>,

  </p>
